About Us

Welcome to Morning & Me, your cozy coffee haven in the heart of San Luis Obispo. We believe that every great day starts with a perfect cup, whether it’s a bold espresso, a creamy latte, or a slow-sipped pour-over.

More than just coffee, we’re about moments—those quiet sunlit mornings, the first sip that sparks inspiration, and the conversations that turn into memories. Our beans are ethically sourced, our brews are crafted with care, and our space is designed to feel like your second home.

So whether you’re fueling up for an adventure, catching up with friends, or just taking a moment for yourself, Morning & Me is here to make your mornings something to savor.

Come sip, stay a while, and start your day with us.
